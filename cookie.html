This website uses cookies to ensure you get the best experience while browsing my portfolio.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve performance, and collect anonymous usage data.

How I Use Cookies

On this site, cookies are used to:

  • Understand how visitors interact with the website (analytics)
  • Ensure the website displays correctly and works smoothly
  • Enhance the overall user experience

    • This portfolio is hosted on GitHub Pages and does not actively collect personal data. However, some cookies might still be used by integrated services like GitHub or analytics tools if added.

    Types of Cookies

    • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the site to function properly
    • Analytics Cookies: Used to track visitor behavior (if enabled)
    • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences like language or theme (if applicable)

    Managing Cookies

    You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can disable them. Note that some features may not work properly without cookies.
